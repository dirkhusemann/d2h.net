Welcome to my blog, d2h.net. Here you will stuff that I find interesting, useful, or noteworthy for some reason or other — in short, stuff the cat dragged in, or the sea delivered to our doorsteps.

This blog is built using

  • hugo,
  • emacs,
  • a derivative of the smigle hugo theme,
  • the computer modern typewriter font, and
  • the special elite typewriter font,

and is hosted via github.
