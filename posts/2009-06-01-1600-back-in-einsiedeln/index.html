16:00 back in einsiedeln

Posted on 2009-06-01 20:34:23 UTC • 91 words • 1 minute read
Tags: life, living in switzerland, biberbrugg, einsiedeln, lucerene

at lucerne verkehrshaus i disembark one station earlier than planned, trying to catch the voralpenexpress train back to biberbrugg. with a bit of, let’s say, fast-hobbling i manage to catch the train and am on my way back home.

at biberbrugg i meet up with mrs d who has just been dropped of by fredi and is tired but happy from a fantastic day out. together we hop on to the train to einsiedeln and walk/hobble home
